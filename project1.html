THREE MUST-SEE PLACES TO VISIT

Each person has special places to share with others. You will see mine in the following.

1. Akdamar Island

  • Location: Van, TURKEY
  • Best time to go: April
  • What to do: Just sit under the blooming almond trees and watch the blue sky and blue Van Lake while the almond flowers raining over you. There is also the Cathedral of Holy Cross that you can visit.
  • For more information: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akdamar_Island

2. Anadolu Kavagi

  • Location: Istanbul, TURKEY
  • Best time to go: Summer
  • What to do: Climb up the Yoros Castle in a shiny summer day. It requires time and energy to reach the top. You will not regret when you see the gorgeous scenery. In front of Yoros Castle the Bosphorus ends and the Black Sea appears with its glory.
  • For more information: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anadolukavagi

3. Niagara Falls

  • Location: Ontario, Canada
  • Best time to go: Summer
  • What to do: Stunningly beautiful scenery. Do not forget to watch it at night. A very different and unbelievable experience.
  • For more information: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niagara_Falls
